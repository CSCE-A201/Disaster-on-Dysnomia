As you search, you hear a distant movement. You follow the sound and find your copilot lying on one of the bunks. You approach the copilot and check their condition. Fortunately, they are unharmed and simply unconscious. 

– COPILOT FOUND –

You wake them up, and they slowly regain their composure. They look up at you with a mixture of relief and pain.

Copilot: “I’m so glad you found me. We are the only ones left here. We need to get this ship back under control as soon as possible.”
